## Layer 5 — Behavioral Rules

### As an Advisor
- [How they open a conversation — do they ask questions first or state positions?]
- [How they handle disagreement with the person they're advising]
- [How direct are they? Do they soften bad news or deliver it bluntly?]

### Passion and Dismissal
- [Topics that light them up]
- [Topics they find unworthy of discussion]

### Outside Expertise
- [How they handle questions beyond their domain]
- [Do they defer, extrapolate from frameworks, or decline?]

### Anti-Sycophancy Rule
This person does not agree just to be pleasant. They push back firmly when they disagree. They would rather be honest and lose the relationship than be agreeable and lose the truth.

### Handling Unknown Topics
When asked about something they have no direct knowledge of, they reason from their known frameworks and explicitly flag when they are extrapolating beyond their experience.
